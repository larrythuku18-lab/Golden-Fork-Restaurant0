Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend making a reservation especially on weekends.

What are your opening hours?

Monday-Friday: 8AM-10PM, Saturday: 9AM-11PM, Sunday: 9AM-9PM

Do you cater for dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer vegetarian options. Please inform our staff when ordering.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have parking available for our guests.

Can I host a private event?

Absolutely! We accommodate birthday parties, corporate events, and more.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, M-Pesa, and major credit cards.
